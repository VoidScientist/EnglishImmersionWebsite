Blueprint

  Below are images of what our project will look like. It is an underground container that will store and clean flood water, to then distribute it via a tap.

It will have multiple section :

  - one that has a filter on top, removing dirt from water and can hold around 400k liters of water.

  - a boiling compartment that will cleanse the water from pathogenic bacterias.

  - a tap to retrieve water from with a heat sensor to prevent burns.

  - solar panels that aren't on the picture under, but are used to power the heating compartment.
