How It Works

  SomaWatt will have 3 safety measures. It is composed of 1 filter, 2 cylinder, 1 pH sensor and solar panels.

  The system will have 2 modes, one to filter and collect the water and one to store it

  When the heavy rains arrives we will put SomaWatt in the first mode, the population will have to open the top of the storing cylinder and add a filter. The filter is there to limit the quantity of dirt that will enter it. Once the rainy season has passed and droughts hit the country, the population can start using the water stored. This is the second mode. We have 3 safety measures, the first one being the big filter, the 2nd one being a pH sensor to check the pH of the water and the last one is a heating cylinder to boil every bacteria to make the water safe to drink.

Material And Capacity

  The whole system will be composed of plastic (PCV) and non rusting metal such as polypropylene, PVDF, bronze and stainless steel.
