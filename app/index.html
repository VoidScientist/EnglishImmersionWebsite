The Project

  Somalia is a country plagued by many issues. Years of civil war have divided the country. Many successive rainless seasons brought massive droughts. Yet despite all this, parts of the country still get flooded. Now, the Somali population suffer from famines, dehydration and a general lack of healthcare.

  As engineers, we plan to address the water issues by building underground containers aiming to store flooded water, to be reused during droughts.
