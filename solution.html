Problem

So our problem is that Somali people can't properly access a water source. There are two main problems with that:

  - People have difficulties to use clean water in their daily life.

  - It causes a second problem, healthcare issue. Indeed, if people can't properly access water, they cannot drink enough, wash their food, their hands as much as they need. They may use dirty water but the consequences are the same. People will get sick more often and diseases will propagate much more between people.

Related SDGs

  That is why our solution are related with the sixth SDG "Clean Water and sanitation" and the third "Good health and well-being". SomaWatt will give a proper access to Somali people, and make life in Somalia healthier for everyone. Moreover we will plant trees so that the project is fully sustainable, so that Somalia gets water even if they do not maintain the containers.
